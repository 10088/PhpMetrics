About the author

Hello, I'm Jean-François Lépine. I'm french consultant, specialized in software quality, industrialization and PHP.

I've created PhpMetrics for many reasons :

  • to get a tool that fits my needs ;
  • to avoid to calculate metrics manually (excellent tools like PhpDepend, PMD... do not provide lot of metrics) ;
  • to provide clear and readable indicators, even for non-specialists ;
  • for fun :)
